# CV8.E5 — Deployment & Support Runbook

**CV:** [CV8 — Customer Readiness](../index.md)
**Status:** ⚪ Planned

---

## What This Is

The operational manual that lets any team member deploy a pilot, support a customer, and respond to an incident — without bottlenecking on a single person's knowledge.

---

## Stories

| Code | Story | Status |
|------|-------|--------|
| CV8.E5.S1 | Pilot deployment runbook (Fly / Hetzner / small K8s) | 📥 Backlog |
| CV8.E5.S2 | Backup & restore procedure documented | 📥 Backlog |
| CV8.E5.S3 | Incident response playbook (on-call rotation, severity levels) | 📥 Backlog |
| CV8.E5.S4 | One-page support guides per common customer question | 📥 Backlog |
| CV8.E5.S5 | Customer onboarding playbook from contract signing to "live" | 📥 Backlog |

---

## Done Condition

- Any team member can execute a pilot deployment from the runbook.
- Backup / restore has been rehearsed against a real DB snapshot.
- The first 10 customer support tickets each have a documented one-page answer.

---

## Out of Scope

- SOC 2 audit packaging — **CV15.E1**.
- 24x7 support — Future.
